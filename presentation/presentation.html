Does a Tiny Speed Advantage Make a Difference during the Race?

In short, not really.

When is this speed difference most important?

We would expect this slight advantage in speed to matter most in the qualifying races since this determines the starting position of the actual race.

Qualifying races are purely a measure of a racer’s speed
